Asteroids

Instructions

Pilot your ship with either W,A,S,D or the Arrow keys. Press SPACE to shoot a laser. If your ship is yellow then you are immune. You will not take damage but you cannot shoot. You will become mortal again 3 seconds after the beginning of the game or after you lose a life.

If you collide with an asteroid -- you lose a life. Hit 0 lives and you lose. However, if you shoot an asteroid with your laser it will either split or disappear (depending on the size of the asteroid).

When asteroids collide they break. However, if the asteroid is a small asteroid then it will be destroyed.

Destroy all of the asteroids on the screen and advance to the next level + gain a life. Score is gained by advancing in level or shooting asteroids.
